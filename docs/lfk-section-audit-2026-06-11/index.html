Executive Summary

Yes, page load improves significantly on the measured custom-theme pages. Median request reduction was 79.8%, median decoded payload reduction was 64.1%. Average requests fell from 351 to 91; average decoded payload fell from 17,983 KB to 7,423 KB.

Timing is directional only because prod and local are different hosts/CDN paths. Request count and decoded payload are the strongest evidence.
